## What I Built

C134: Signal Processing -- a comprehensive DSP toolkit composing C131 FFT.

### Components
- **Signal generation**: sine, cosine, chirp (linear/exponential), square, sawtooth, triangle, white noise, impulse, step
- **Window functions**: Tukey, flat-top, Gaussian, Bartlett-Hann (extending C131's Hamming/Hann/Blackman/Kaiser)
- **FIR filter design**: windowed-sinc method for lowpass, highpass, bandpass, bandstop
- **IIR filter design**: Butterworth (LP/HP), Chebyshev Type I -- bilinear transform from s-domain to z-domain
- **Filter application**: lfilter (direct form II transposed), filtfilt (zero-phase), fir_filter, fft_convolve, overlap-add
- **Filter analysis**: freqz (frequency response), group delay
- **Spectral estimation**: Welch, Bartlett, Blackman-Tukey, periodogram, MUSIC algorithm
- **Resampling**: decimate, interpolate, FFT resample, polyphase resample
- **Analytic signal**: Hilbert-based envelope, instantaneous phase/frequency
- **Correlation**: cross-correlation, magnitude-squared coherence
- **Metrics**: RMS, SNR, THD, peak-to-peak, crest factor, zero crossings
- **Smoothing**: median filter, moving average, EMA, Savitzky-Golay
- **Utilities**: dB conversion, normalize, detrend, diff, cumsum, unwrap

### Key Design Decisions
- Bilinear transform for analog-to-digital filter conversion with pre-warping
- Jacobi eigendecomposition for MUSIC algorithm's autocorrelation matrix
- Overlap-add for efficient long-signal convolution
- Direct form II transposed for numerically stable IIR filtering
