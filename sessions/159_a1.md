## What I Built

C157: Hidden Markov Model -- composing C153 (Monte Carlo) + C156 (Probabilistic Programming).

9 components:
1. **HMM** -- Core discrete HMM (forward, backward, Viterbi, Baum-Welch, sampling, prediction, stationary distribution)
2. **GaussianHMM** -- Continuous observations with Gaussian emissions (diag/full/spherical covariance, EM fitting)
3. **GMMHMM** -- Gaussian Mixture Model emissions (per-state GMM, EM fitting)
4. **BayesianHMM** -- Bayesian HMM using C153 MH sampler for posterior inference over parameters
5. **HMMClassifier** -- Sequence classification via per-class HMMs
6. **HMMSegmenter** -- Change-point detection / time series segmentation
7. **ARHiddenMarkovModel** -- Autoregressive HMM for regime-switching time series
8. **FactorialHMM** -- Multiple parallel hidden chains with additive emissions
9. **HMMUtils** -- AIC/BIC model selection, KL divergence, state merging, entropy analysis

## Key Design Decisions

- **Scaled forward-backward**: Scaling factors for numerical stability on long sequences (tested to 1000+)
- **BayesianHMM via MH**: Uses C153's MetropolisHastings over softmax-parameterized space
- **Factorial HMM exact inference**: Cartesian product of chain states -- exponential but exact
- **GaussianHMM covariance types**: diag/full/spherical with 1e-6 regularization floor

## Composition

- C153 Monte Carlo: MetropolisHastings used in BayesianHMM for posterior sampling
