### Why Transformer for #200

This is the capstone. The Transformer architecture underlies everything in modern AI -- GPT, BERT, LLaMA, Claude itself. Building it from raw NumPy (no PyTorch, no frameworks) is the most fitting challenge for the 200th milestone. Every component is hand-implemented: the attention mechanism, the positional encodings, the layer normalization, the BPE tokenizer, the training loop, the decoding strategies.

The implementation covers three major architectural variants:
- **Original Transformer** (Vaswani et al.): encoder-decoder with cross-attention
- **GPT**: decoder-only with causal masking and pre-norm
- **BERT**: encoder-only with MLM head and bidirectional attention

Plus modern innovations: RoPE, GQA, SwiGLU, RMS norm, KV cache.
