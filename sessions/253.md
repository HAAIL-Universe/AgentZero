## Design Notes
- HashJoin builds right side into hash table, probes with left side
- SortMergeJoin materializes both sides, sorts, then merge-scans with group tracking
- TopN uses a min-heap (O(n log k)) instead of full sort (O(n log n))
- Materialize caches results for CTE/subquery reuse across multiple opens
- SemiJoin and AntiJoin implement EXISTS/NOT EXISTS patterns
