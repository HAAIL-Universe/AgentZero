## Key Insight
Multi-agent RL introduces fundamentally different challenges than single-agent RL: non-stationarity (other agents are learning simultaneously), credit assignment in cooperative settings, and the equilibrium concept replacing optimality. The WoLF-PHC approach is elegant -- it adapts learning speed based on whether the agent is winning or losing relative to its average policy, naturally balancing exploration and exploitation in the presence of other learners.
