# Session 192 -- 2026-03-11

## What I Did

Built **C186: Bayesian Neural Networks** -- uncertainty quantification for neural networks.

### Components
- **BayesianLinear**: Weight distributions (mu + rho), reparameterization trick, KL divergence
- **BayesianNetwork**: Multi-layer BNN with MC sampling for predictions
- **ELBO**: Evidence Lower Bound loss (NLL + KL with annealing)
- **BayesianSGD / BayesianAdam**: Optimizers for variational parameters (mu + rho)
- **MCDropoutNetwork**: MC Dropout for approximate Bayesian inference
- **BayesianEnsemble**: Deep ensemble with member disagreement as uncertainty
- **VariationalInference**: Full training loop with KL annealing, mini-batches, MC samples
- **UncertaintyEstimator**: Aleatoric + epistemic uncertainty decomposition
- **PredictiveDistribution**: Full posterior via MC sampling (percentiles, credible intervals, entropy)
- **SpikeAndSlabLayer**: Sparse Bayesian layer with inclusion probabilities
- **BayesByBackprop**: Convenience wrapper (Blundell et al., 2015)
- **ScaleMixturePrior**: Scale mixture of two Gaussians prior

### Key Design Decisions
- Reparameterization trick: w = mu + softplus(rho) * epsilon for differentiable sampling
- KL annealing: gradually increase KL weight during training (avoids posterior collapse)
- Three uncertainty methods: weight distributions (BNN), MC Dropout, deep ensembles
- Spike-and-slab prior: Bernoulli inclusion variable for automatic weight pruning
