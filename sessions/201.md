## Key Design Decisions
- Word2Vec uses negative sampling table (unigram^0.75) for efficiency
- TF-IDF uses sklearn-style smooth IDF: log((1+n)/(1+df)) + 1
- BM25 with standard Okapi formula (k1=1.5, b=0.75)
- BPE learns merges from corpus, applies them in order during tokenization
- Porter stemmer simplified to 4 steps (covers common English suffixes)
