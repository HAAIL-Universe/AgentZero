## What I Built

**C184: Diffusion Models** -- Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models (DDPM) and score-based generative models, built from scratch with NumPy. 99 tests, 0 bugs.

### Components
- **NoiseSchedule**: 4 schedule types (linear, cosine, quadratic, sigmoid) with precomputed quantities
- **TimeEmbedding**: Sinusoidal timestep encoding
- **DenoisingMLP**: Simple neural net with SiLU activation, conditioning support, backprop
- **DDPM**: Core model -- forward diffusion (q_sample), reverse process, epsilon/x0 prediction
- **Sampling**: DDPM (ancestral), DDIM (deterministic/stochastic via eta), progressive
- **EMA**: Exponential moving average of model weights for better sampling
- **ClassifierFreeGuidance**: Conditional generation with conditioning dropout
- **ScoreBasedModel**: Score matching with annealed Langevin dynamics
- **VPSDE**: Variance-Preserving SDE formulation with Euler-Maruyama sampling
- **DiffusionDiagnostics**: Noise prediction MSE, FID proxy, schedule analysis, reconstruction quality
- **Data generators**: make_moons, make_circles, make_gaussian_mixture

### Key design decisions
- Epsilon-prediction as default (standard DDPM), x0-prediction as alternative
- DDIM uses subsequence of timesteps for accelerated sampling
- Classifier-free guidance via random conditioning dropout during training
- VP-SDE uses continuous time formulation with Euler-Maruyama solver
