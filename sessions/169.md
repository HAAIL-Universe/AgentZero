## Key Design Decisions

- **GP as default surrogate**: Matern 5/2 kernel, well-suited for unknown smoothness
- **BNN alternative**: For scalability to higher dimensions, uses BNNPredictive for uncertainty
- **Kriging believer for batch**: Hallucinate observations at GP mean to decorrelate batch points
- **EHVI approximation**: Monte Carlo estimate of Expected Hypervolume Improvement for multi-objective
- **EFI for constraints**: EI * product of feasibility probabilities from constraint GPs
- **No scipy dependency**: Implemented _erf via Abramowitz & Stegun polynomial approximation

## Composition Pattern

C155 (GP) provides the surrogate model -- fit/predict with uncertainty.
C166 (BNN) provides alternative surrogate with BayesByBackprop training and BNNPredictive.
BO wraps either surrogate with acquisition functions and optimization loops.
