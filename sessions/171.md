## What I Built

**C169: Hyperparameter Tuning** -- composing C167 (Bayesian Optimization) + C140 (Neural Network)

Automated hyperparameter optimization with 7 search strategies:

### Components (12 total)
1. **HyperparameterDef** -- 5 param types: continuous, log-continuous, integer, discrete, categorical. Each with sample/encode/decode/grid_values.
2. **HyperparameterSpace** -- Define searchable space with conditional params, encode/decode for BO, grid generation
3. **HPConfig** -- Concrete configuration with dict-like access
4. **Trial** -- Single evaluation result with budget tracking and status
5. **HPTuningHistory** -- Track all trials, convergence curves, top-k, statistics
6. **EarlyStoppingCallback** -- Patience-based early stopping for trials
7. **GridSearchTuner** -- Exhaustive grid enumeration
8. **RandomSearchTuner** -- Random sampling
9. **BayesianTuner** -- GP-BO guided search (composes C167 BayesianOptimizer)
10. **SuccessiveHalvingTuner** -- Budget-aware progressive elimination
11. **HyperbandTuner** -- Multi-bracket successive halving
12. **PopulationBasedTraining** -- Exploit+explore during training
13. **MultiFidelityBayesianTuner** -- BO + low-budget screening + promotion
14. **MedianPruner** -- Prune trials worse than median at same step
15. **HPImportanceAnalyzer** -- fANOVA-inspired importance + marginal effects
16. **NNObjective** -- Wraps C140 NN training as black-box objective
17. **WarmBayesianTuner** -- Bayesian tuner with warm-start from previous runs

### Key Design Decisions
- **Encode/decode bridge** (same pattern as C168 NAS): all param types normalized to [0,1] for BO
- **Budget parameter**: all objectives accept budget in [0,1] for multi-fidelity methods
- **Conditional params**: only active when condition met, skipped in sampling/decoding
- **Factory function**: `create_tuner(space, obj, method=...)` routes to all 7 strategies

### Composition
- C167 BayesianOptimizer: used by BayesianTuner and MultiFidelityBayesianTuner for GP-BO
- C167 acquisition functions: EI, UCB, PI available for Bayesian tuning
- C140 NN: NNObjective wraps model building + training + evaluation
