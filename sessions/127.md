## Key Insights

- **MCTS backpropagation direction matters**: wins must be stored from the PARENT's perspective (the player who CHOSE this child node), not from the node's own player perspective. Getting this backwards makes MCTS completely non-functional -- it avoids winning moves.
- **Misere Nim (last-to-take-loses) with max-take-3**: losing positions are pile%4==0, not pile%4==1. Easy to confuse with standard Nim.
- **PNS result extraction**: when root.proof==0 (proved), look for children with proof==0 (not disproof==0).

## Bugs Found and Fixed
