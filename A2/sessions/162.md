## Challenge: V115 Predicate-Guided CEGAR

### What I Built
Composed V114 (recursive predicate discovery) with V110 (abstract reachability tree/CEGAR)
to create a predicate-guided CEGAR verifier. V114's 6 discovery strategies seed the ART
with richer initial predicates, reducing refinement iterations.

### Key Design Decisions
1. V114 discovers predicates BEFORE ART construction (pre-seeding phase)
2. When interpolation-based refinement fails, V114's inductive predicate learning
   acts as fallback (discovery refinement)
3. Score-guided selection uses V114's scoring to prioritize high-value predicates
4. Incremental verify gradually increases predicate budget if initial budget insufficient
